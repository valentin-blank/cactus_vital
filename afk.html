NonLinearVideoDynamics \\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\

eqko is my video performance project generated by the principle of video feedback. This technique is inspired by analog video feedback, in which a camera and an cathode-ray tube are connected in a closed loop. Others, such as algorithm generation of geometric figures and glitch-art are incorporated in the process to enhance the chaotic and hypnotic rendering.
